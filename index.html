Meet the Teacher

More Than a Teacher — A Champion for Every Student

#1 Group
Leader

Mrs. Amy Hartley is an English Language Arts teacher at William B. Travis High School in Fort Bend ISD — one of the most diverse public high schools in Texas. She didn't just fall into teaching; she was drawn to the power of stories, words, and the human connection that lives inside them.

"I became a teacher because I truly loved English Language Arts. But once I stepped into the classroom, I realized it was about so much more — building confidence, helping students find their voice, and walking alongside them as they grow into who they're becoming."

Teaching isn't just her career — it's her calling. Every day she shows up for students who are still discovering who they are, helping them believe in themselves when no one else has.

One of her most powerful moments? Watching a quiet student who "wasn't good at English" deliver a thesis so powerful it silenced the room. By year's end, he was leading class discussions. That's Mrs. Hartley's impact.
